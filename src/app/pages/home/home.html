Kismathpur Plot Owners’ Community

Representing plot owners who purchased between 1997 — 2000

Nestled near the serene banks of the River Musi, our layout in Kismathpur stands as a testament to trust, perseverance, and unity.

Formed by owners who purchased plots between 1997 and 2000, this community represents decades of shared vision for rightful ownership and peaceful living.

Our mission is to bring together all plot owners, preserve our collective rights, and ensure the land’s legal clarity for generations to come.

Here, you’ll find verified information, court proceedings, government records, and updates related to our survey numbers of Kismathpur Village.

Together, we stand for transparency, justice, and rightful possession.

Join hands with fellow owners, stay informed, and be part of the movement to protect and reclaim what is rightfully ours.

Let’s build not just homes — but trust, community, and a shared legacy.
